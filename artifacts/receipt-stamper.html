Checkpoint Rings

      
When you paste a receipt into a mark, consider including one or two “checkpoint rings”: a status code, a commit hash, or a short quote. These are the hooks that let others verify without trusting your memory.

    


    

      
Anchor Stones

      
An anchor is the stable point you can always return to: a repository URL, a known-good commit, or a workaround that still functions when the main entrance is dark.
